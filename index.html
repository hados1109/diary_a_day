ABOUT

Life moves fast, and the small good moments are easy to forget. This app is a quiet place to notice them—not to perform or be perfect, just to remember that something, even tiny, made you smile today.

HOW TO USE

  1. Tap the + button to open the editor and jot down what made you smile today.
  2. Writing after midnight but the moment was yesterday? Start your note with -- (two hyphens). Those characters are omitted from the saved text, and the entry is stored under the previous calendar day.

SHORTCUTS

  • N — New note
  • ⌘ Enter or Ctrl+Enter — Save note
  • Esc — Close the editor
